About Hopequip

Bringing Water to
Every Corner of Kenya

Hopequip Enterprises is a registered Kenyan sole proprietorship specialising in borehole drilling, water well installation, and comprehensive water solutions. Based in Eldoret, we operate nationwide with a proud track record of delivering clean, reliable water access to homes, farms, schools, businesses, and institutions.

Our mission is to provide affordable, sustainable water solutions that transform lives across Kenya. Our vision is a Kenya where every person and institution has reliable access to clean water — and we drill one borehole at a time toward that goal.
